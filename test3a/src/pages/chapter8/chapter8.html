8.2 | Acknowledgements

St. Cuthbert’s Hospice:


St. Cuthbert’s Hospice in Durham offers a range of services, not only to care for individuals through the different stages of illness, but to support family and friends. St. Cuthbert’s Hospice has kindly agreed to share their amazing work ‘Everything in Place’ and support this to be developed in to a useful ‘App’ that many more people can benefit from. Without their support this app would not have been possible.

North East NHS Code4health Community:


The North East community have supported this fantastic idea and put the concept forward to be built in to an app. The group is made up of local CCG’s, health partners, public sector, Voluntary organisations and some Tech companies. Particular acknowledgement goes to NHS Sunderland CCG, NHS Innovations and North Tyneside CCG. They have provided the guidance and support to help this app be developed.
